About School

Welcome to GOVT. HIGH SCH. ULDHAN

Established in 2001, GOVT. HIGH SCH. ULDHAN serves students from classes 6 to 10 in a co-educational secondary learning environment.

The school is managed by the Department of Education and supports academic growth, discipline, and equal access for learners in the rural Kharkhoda block area.

Education is a powerful foundation for success, and teachers help children grow into socially, mentally, and intellectually capable individuals.
