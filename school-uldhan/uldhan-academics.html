Academic Overview

Structured learning for foundational and secondary-level growth.

Grades Offered

Class 6, Class 7, Class 8, Class 9, and Class 10 under government school framework.

Instruction Language

Hindi is used as the primary medium of classroom teaching and communication.

Classroom System

Regular classroom teaching with lesson-based revision and periodic assessments.

Assessment Pattern

Unit tests, periodic internal evaluations, and term examinations.

Departments and Subject Areas

Core subject groups supporting classes 6 to 10.

Languages

Hindi language proficiency, reading comprehension, and writing skills.

Mathematics

Concept-based math, numerical reasoning, and problem solving.

Science

Foundational physical and life science with classroom demonstrations.

Social Science

History, geography, civics, and social awareness through practical examples.

English Support

Basic English reading, vocabulary, and communication support modules.

Physical Education

Sports activity and fitness routines using the school playground.

Academic Calendar (Indicative)

Month-wise planning for smooth session execution.

  • April-May

  • July-August

  • November-January

    Advanced syllabus completion and exam practice.

  • February-March

    Final exams, closure, and result preparation.

Learning Support Focus

  • Regular attendance tracking and follow-up.
  • Class-wise revision modules before exams.
  • Teacher-parent communication for learning gaps.
  • Special support for foundational literacy and numeracy.
  • School assembly-based value education and discipline.
