School History

Serving rural education in Meerut district since 2001.

GOVT. HIGH SCH. ULDHAN was established in 2001 and is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in the rural area of Kharkhoda block in Meerut district, Uttar Pradesh.

The school offers classes from grade 6 to grade 10 in a co-educational setting. Hindi is the medium of instruction, and the school remains accessible through all-weather road connectivity.

Mission and Vision

Grounded learning for every child, with equal access and disciplined growth.

Mission

Provide affordable, inclusive, and reliable secondary education for rural families while supporting academic and personal development for each student.

Vision

Build confident learners who are prepared for higher studies, social responsibility, and meaningful contribution to their communities.

School Values

Respect, discipline, punctuality, gender equality, and support for every child through a safe and structured school environment.

Community Focus

Encourage parent participation, local engagement, and healthy student habits through awareness programs and PTM interactions.

Leadership

School leadership works with teachers and parents to sustain quality education.

Headmaster's Office

The school has a separate room for Headmaster/Teacher coordination and academic planning.

Academic Team

Teachers coordinate annual planning, classroom delivery, and student evaluation as per school calendar.

Parent Partnership

Periodic meetings are conducted to review attendance, learning progress, discipline, and support needs.

Administrative Support

Admission records, notices, and documentation are maintained with office-level support during school hours.
