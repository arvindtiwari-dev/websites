Admission Process

Simple process for student enrollment in classes 6 to 10.

  • Step 1: Enquiry and Eligibility Check

    Confirm class availability and required documents at school office.

  • Step 2: Fill Application Form

    Submit student and parent details through online form or office counter.

  • Step 3: Document Verification

    Carry original documents for school verification and approval.

  • Step 4: Class Allotment

    Class section is assigned based on seat availability and records.

  • Step 5: Orientation and Start

    Student joins class and receives timetable, books list, and notices.

Eligibility

  • Admissions available for classes 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10.
  • Student should meet age and previous class qualification criteria.
  • Transfer certificate required for inter-school admissions where applicable.

Documents Required

  • Previous class mark sheet
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Birth date proof
  • Student photo (passport size)
  • Parent/guardian identity proof
  • Address proof

Online Admission Form

Submit details online, then complete verification at school office.

Note: Online submission is the first step. Admission is confirmed only after school-level document verification.
