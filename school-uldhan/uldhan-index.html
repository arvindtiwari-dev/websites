About GOVT. HIGH SCH. ULDHAN

Established in 2001, the school serves students from class 6 to class 10 in a co-educational Hindi-medium environment.

The school is located in the rural area of Kharkhoda block, Meerut district. It has a government building, functional classrooms, drinking water through hand pumps, separate toilets for boys and girls, and a playground for co-curricular growth.

Academic session begins in April every year, and the school provides mid-day meals prepared within school premises.
